Formulas

  • mass = density * volume
  • height = max height - distance
  • acceleration = velocity / time
  • PE = mass * gravity * height
  • KE = 1/2 * mass * (velocity * velocity)
  • ME = PE + KE
  • Force = mass * acceleration
  • Work = Force * distance
